Frequently Asked Questions

Go to "Explore Places" from your dashboard, browse through our curated destinations, select a place that interests you, and follow the simple booking process. You can choose your travel dates, number of people, and customize your trip.
Click on "My Bookings" in your dashboard to view all your booking history and current status. You can see upcoming trips, completed journeys, and track the status of each booking in real-time.
Click on "Book Hotels" from your dashboard, search for hotels by place or destination, browse through available options, and complete the booking form with your check-in/check-out dates and room requirements.
We currently accept cash payments. Your booking will be confirmed immediately, and our tour guide will contact you for payment details and arrangements. We're working on adding more payment options soon.
For booking modifications or cancellations, please contact our support team through the contact information below. Our team will assist you with the process based on our cancellation policy and the specific terms of your booking.
